DID YOU KNOW

Did you know that the average person checks their phone over 96 times a day—that’s once every 10 to 12 minutes? Constant digital stimulation can lead to mental fatigue, reduced attention span, and even brain fog. Taking short breaks from screens, practicing mindfulness, and reconnecting with offline activities can dramatically improve your focus and mental clarity. Your brain craves rest—give it the space to reset.
